Who we are

We are B-bike.

Established in 2020 and ready to shake the field of e-bikes. Our mission is to make electric bikes accessible to all. We think that everyone with a heart for the environment should be able to ride an e-bike. But don't worry, even though our products are competitively priced our quality is top notch. Our Motto: Alone you can ride fast but with us you can ride far.

Our team

At B-bike diversity is not only something for the books.

Social engagement

At B-bike we love to give back. Beside our give-back-a-tree project we also donate 5% of our profits to other social projects.
