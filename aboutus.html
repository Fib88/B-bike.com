Who we are

We are B-bike.

Established in 2020 and ready to shake the field of e-bikes. Our mission is to make electric bikes accessible to all. We think that everyone with a heart for the environment should be able to ride an e-bike. But don't worry, even though our products are competitively priced our quality is top notch.
