Discover our bikes

The bike

B-bike have been designed to offer optimal comfort in the streets of Antwerpen. Its wide saddle, large wheels and front suspension help to tame cobblestones and tram rails. Thanks to its electric assistance up to 25 km / h, you won’t feel the hills of Antwerpen.

In conjunction with your B-bike hire, you can also hire equipment to transport your children.

Sustainability

Through developing personalised solutions that facilitate and encourage people to transition to cycling, B-bike contributes to a higher quality of life.

We manufacture all of our products with clean and renewable energy provided by the sun from the PV farm that we own. By owning a B-Bike you become a part of the community that cares for the sustainability of our planet.

Cycle repair and workshop services

Our fully equipped workshops are class leading. We handle all bicycle repairs from simple puncture fixes through to complex builds and repairs. Our mechanics are Cytech-qualified and trained on Bromptons, hub gears and electronic drivetrains as well as more unusual repairs..

For help, information or to make a booking please go to our contact form.

Maintenance

We are a full-service bike repair shop. From fixing a flat tire to a comprehensive tune-up, we can have you road-ready in no time.

Quality

With inspiration and innovation, combined with quality and value, we aim to improve the lives of our customers.

Payment

For the purchase of your B-bike, we offer you payment facility. You have the opportunity to pay in three times without fees.
